SoAR 2025

We are pleased to invite you to participate in the 6th SoAR Symposium, scheduled to take place on March 20th and 21st, 2025, in the beautiful city of Bari, Italy. This prestigious event will be hosted at the University of Bari, thanks to Prof. Angela Stefania Bergantino.

The SoAR 2025 scientific committee welcomes the submission of high-quality contributions focusing on a wide variety of aviation topics. The SoAR Symposium is being launched as a research forum grounded in the fields of Operations Research and Economics to analyze aviation markets and push the boundaries of knowledge forward. The symposia will consist of:

(1) invited talks by two distinguished keynote speakers;

(2) extended presentations and discussions of consolidated research;

(3) early-stage presentations of research intentions.
